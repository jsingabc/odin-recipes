Oatmeal Recipe

Description

This recipe is for making my oatmeal breakfast. It does not take much effort really to make, just some imgaination. Here are the steps to make it.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Instructions

  1. Pour 1/2 cup oats in bowl
  2. add enough water to be just above oats
  3. Microwave until oats bubble 90s
  4. Add milk and sugar
