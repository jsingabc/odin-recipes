Salad Recipe

Description

This recipe is for making my favorite homemade salad. It does not take much effort really to make, just some imgaination. Here are the steps to make it.

Ingredients

  • large plastic container with cover
  • lettuce romaine cut
  • mini tomatoes
  • bacon bits
  • light ranch dressing
  • salt, pepper
  • canned tuna

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients into bowl
  2. be sure to add just enough of each by feel
  3. shake the bowl until mixed
  4. enjoy your salad
