Egg Omelet Recipe

Description

This recipe is for making my favorite egg Omelet. It does not take much effort really to make, just some imgaination. Here are the steps to make it.

Ingredients

  • Cheddar cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • cut meat any
  • bacon bits
  • salt, pepper
  • frying pan
  • spray grease

Instructions

  1. Get frying pan warm and add grease
  2. break 3 eggs and scramble in pot
  3. when eggs half cook add all other ingredients
  4. fold egg over each half
  5. when cheese it melted take the omelet out
